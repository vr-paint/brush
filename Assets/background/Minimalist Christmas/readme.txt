Thank you for downloading our Minimalist Christmas pack!

Minimalist Christmas is a pack that contains low poly objects following the Christmas theme.

Most models share the same material (Palette), so you can edit them with more convenience in your preferred 3D editor.

This pack contains:
- 1 Demo scene
- 12 materials
- 18 fbx models
- 23 prefabs
